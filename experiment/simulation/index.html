Instructions
  1. Click on the button "Table of molecular weights of amino acid" to see the 1-letter code and corresponding molecular weight. Click on "Table of pka and pI values" to see the specific values for each amino acid. Click on respective buttons to hide the table.
  2. Enter any Amino acid sequence in the input box and click on the button "Submit".
    • Approximate molecular weight of the sequence
      1. Enter number of amino acid in the input box.
      2. Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
    • Actual molecular weight of the sequence
      1. Enter the calculated molecular weight of amino acid in the input box.
      2. Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
    • Molar extinction coefficient of peptide sequence
      1. Enter number W, Y and C in the respective input boxes.
      2. Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
  3. Isoelectric point (pI) Calculation
  4. Enter DNA sequence in the input box 1 and enter compliment of the first DNA sequence. Click on the "Submit" button.
    • Approximate molecular weight of double-standed DNA sequence
      1. Enter number of bases from single strand DNA sequence in the input box.
      2. Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
    • Actual molecular weight of double-stranded DNA sequence
      1. Enter total number of A, T, C, G from the double strand DNA in the respective input boxes
      2. Next Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
    • Melting temperature (Tm) of double-stranded DNA sequence
      1. Enter the melting temperature of the DNA sequence in the input box "Enter calculated Tm".
      2. Click on "Check" button to display a message indicating correctness. To reveal the correct answer, click on the "Show Answer" button.
    • Click on "Plot Tm Vs GC" button.
Note: To repeat the experiment, click on the "Re-Do the Experiment" button.
